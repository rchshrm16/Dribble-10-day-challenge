The Legend of Tarzan

by David Yates

The Legend of Tarzan is bursting with big ideas: animal vs. human, exploration vs. exploitation, primitivism vs. civilization .... It's a heavy thematic load for a sinfle movie to handle - especially this one,which nearly collapses from burden. Nut it's hard to fault Director Davic Yates, who captained the last four Harry Potter movies, for having ambition.
